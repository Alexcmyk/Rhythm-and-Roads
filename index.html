About R&R.

R&R opened in the Summer of 2020 with the founder, Olivia Taphorn, just finishing up at Berklee College of Music. Olivia’s experience in working for a music publisher as well as in music curation, licensing and sync shaped what R&R is today.

The company has built relationships with top music libraries, publishers and labels all around the world to provide the best & most creative options for their clients.
